TOP 10 PLACES TO VISIT IN MALAYSIA SELAY

1. PETRONAS TWIN TOWER

The Petronas Twin Towers are iconic landmarks in Kuala Lumpur, offering breathtaking views from the observation deck.

2. BATU CAVES

Batu Caves is a series of limestone caves with a massive statue of Lord Murugan, a significant Hindu religious site.

3. KLCC PARK

Located in the heart of the city, KLCC Park is a lush green space offering a beautiful view of the Petronas Towers.

4. THEAN HOU TEMPLE

Thean Hou Temple is one of the largest and oldest temples in Southeast Asia, offering beautiful views and a peaceful atmosphere.

5. MASJID JAMEK SULTAN ABDUL SAMAD

A stunning mosque located at the confluence of two rivers in Kuala Lumpur, known for its Moorish architectural style.

6. KL FOREST ECO PARK

KL Forest Eco Park offers a peaceful escape in the middle of the city, with walking trails and a canopy walkway.

7. TIOMAN ISLAND

Tioman Island is a tropical paradise known for its crystal-clear waters, coral reefs, and beautiful beaches.

8. LANGKAWI ISLAND

Langkawi is a popular island destination with beautiful beaches, a cable car ride to the top of Gunung Mat Cincang, and duty-free shopping.

9. TERENGGANU

Terengganu is known for its scenic beaches, cultural heritage, and the famous Crystal Mosque.

10. TAMAN NEGARA

Taman Negara is one of the world's oldest rainforests, offering jungle treks, river cruises, and wildlife encounters.
